The Mission

An Active Life, Your Way.

Our mission is to enable you to maintain an active lifestyle doing what you enjoy. Modern schedules make that hard — so we built a gym that flexes around you: open 24/7, no contract, and staffed by coaches who meet you where you are.

From competitive athletes to members taking their first steps, Renegade is a place to train hard, feel welcome, and actually stick with it.

  • Founded & run locally in Wauconda, IL.
  • High-performance equipment for powerlifting and hypertrophy alike.
  • Spotless facility — showers, locker rooms, and turf kept clean.
