The Mission

An active life, your way

Our mission is to enable you to maintain an active lifestyle doing what you enjoy. Modern schedules make that hard — so we built a gym that flexes around you: open 24/7, no contract, staffed by coaches who meet you where you are.

  • Founded & run locally

    Owned and operated right here in Wauconda, IL — not a franchise playbook.

  • High-performance equipment

    Built for powerlifting and hypertrophy alike — racks, rigs, and specialty bars.

  • Spotless facility

    Showers, lockers, and turf kept clean — members call it one of the cleanest gyms in Lake County.

Prime Location

Right on Route 12. Minutes from everywhere.

Renegade sits directly on US Route 12 in Wauconda — the artery that ties Lake County together. Most of the county is a 15–20 minute drive, so the gym fits your route instead of the other way around.

  • Easy on, easy off

    Straight shot down Rte 12 / Rand Rd — no back-road maze to get to a workout.

  • Free parking at the door

    Pull in, park, train — no meters, no garages, no circling the block.

  • Open 24/7 for members

    On your commute, after the kids are down, before sunrise — the door's always open.
