Good to Know

Membership, answered

The short version of everything people ask at the front desk.

Is there really no contract?

Really. Every membership is month to month — stay because you love training here, cancel anytime with no penalty and no runaround.

What's the annual fee?

A once-a-year fee alongside your monthly rate: $50 on Individual, $25 on the Community rate. No other hidden charges — ever.

How does 24/7 access work?

You get your own key fob on day one. It opens the door any hour of any day — early mornings, late nights, and holidays included.

When is the gym staffed?

Mon–Thu 8a–8p, Friday 8a–7p, Saturday 8a–2p, and Sunday 8a–12p. Members train around the clock either way.

Can I try it first?

Yes — book a free tour during staffed hours and we'll walk you through the whole floor, no pressure.

What about training add-ons?

Personal training, nutrition coaching, and group classes are add-ons on top of any membership — ask us for current rates.
