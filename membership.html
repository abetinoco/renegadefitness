The Renegade Promise

Why No Contract?

Because a great gym shouldn’t need to trap you. We’d rather earn your membership every single month with a clean floor, working equipment, and coaches who care.

  • No long-term commitment — stay because you want to.
  • No hidden fees — what you see is what you pay.
  • Cancel anytime — no penalty, no runaround.
Questions

Good To Know.

Is it really 24/7?

Yes — members get key-fob access any hour of any day. Staffed hours are Mon–Thu 8a–8p, Fri 8a–7p, Sat 8a–2p, and Sun 8a–12p.

Do you offer discounts?

Our Community rate is for military, police, firefighters, paramedics, and teachers with valid ID.

Is there a contract?

Never. Memberships are month-to-month with no penalty to cancel. The only recurring items are your monthly rate and a small annual membership fee.

Can I try before I join?

Absolutely. Grab a $10 day pass or book a free tour and see the floor for yourself.
